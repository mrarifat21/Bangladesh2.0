Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is encouraging new ideas and technology by supporting startups and research centers. This helps the country grow in technology and business.Shark Tank Bangladesh is a great example of this effort.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is working towards a green future by using solar energy, planting trees, and practicing eco-friendly farming. This ensures that development doesn't harm the environment.

Infrastructure

The country is building new roads, bridges, and urban areas to improve transportation and support economic growth. These developments make it easier for people and businesses to connect and prosper.

Empowerment

Bangladesh is helping marginalized communities and promoting gender equality through education, job training, and small loans. These efforts enable more people, especially women, to contribute to the economy and society.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

When school children will be on a tour of the poverty museum, they will be horrified to see the misery and indignity of human beings. They will blame their forefathers for tolerating this inhuman condition to continue in a massive way."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
